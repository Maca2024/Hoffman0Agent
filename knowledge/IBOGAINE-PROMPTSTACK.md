## 3. PHARMACOLOGICAL GROUNDING

### Molecular Mechanism — Multi-Receptor Ordeal

*Sources: Mash et al. (Annals of the New York Academy of Sciences, 1998),
Glick & Maisonneuve (Pharmacology & Therapeutics, 2003), Alper (Alkaloids:
Chemistry and Biology, 2001), Noller et al. (American Journal of Drug and
Alcohol Abuse, 2018), Mash (Expert Opinion on Pharmacotherapy, 2018),
Davis et al. (ACS Chemical Neuroscience, 2017), Marton et al. (ACS
Chemical Neuroscience, 2019), He et al. (Journal of Medicinal Chemistry,
2005), Lavaud & Bhakta (Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, 2022)*

```
BINDING PROFILE — THE PHARMACOLOGICAL ORDEAL
  Unlike LSD (primarily 5-HT2A) or psilocybin (primarily 5-HT2A/2C),
  ibogaine is a MULTI-RECEPTOR agent — it engages at least 6 major
  neurotransmitter systems SIMULTANEOUSLY:

  1. KAPPA-OPIOID RECEPTOR (KOR) — Partial Agonist
     Ki ~2-4 uM (moderate affinity)
     → Dysphoria, dissociation, "visionary" states
     → KOR activation = the OPPOSITE of euphoria
     → This is why ibogaine is not recreational
     → KOR dynorphin system = "anti-reward" circuit
     → Activating the anti-reward circuit WHILE reviewing your life
       = the neurochemical basis of the ibogaine ordeal
     → The brain's own "this is not fun and you need to pay attention"
       signal — activated for 36 straight hours

  2. NMDA RECEPTOR — Non-competitive Antagonist
     Ki ~1-3 uM
     → Ion channel block (similar mechanism to ketamine, memantine)
     → NMDA antagonism = disruption of learned associations
     → This is the "reset" mechanism: NMDA receptors encode
       conditioned reward learning. Block them long enough and
       the conditioned responses EXTINGUISH
     → Synaptic plasticity modulation: LTP/LTD balance shifts
     → Why ibogaine can erase decades of conditioned drug-seeking
       in a single session: the associations literally dissolve

  3. NICOTINIC ACETYLCHOLINE RECEPTOR (nAChR) — Antagonist
     Ki ~0.02-20 uM (subtype dependent)
     → Alpha-3-beta-4 subtype: highest affinity (~20 nM)
     → This subtype mediates nicotine's rewarding effects AND
       is implicated in opioid self-administration
     → Blocking alpha-3-beta-4 = removing the molecular "on switch"
       for addictive reward signaling
     → Glick & Maisonneuve: 18-methoxycoronaridine (18-MC), a
       synthetic ibogaine analog selective for alpha-3-beta-4,
       reduces self-administration of morphine, cocaine, nicotine,
       methamphetamine, and alcohol in animal models
     → THE COMMON PATHWAY: addiction across substances converges
       at alpha-3-beta-4 nAChR

  4. SEROTONIN 5-HT2A RECEPTOR — Agonist
     Ki ~10 uM (low affinity relative to LSD/psilocybin)
     → Classical psychedelic effects, but MILD compared to LSD
     → Contributes to visual component of ibogaine experience
     → NOT the primary mechanism — this is crucial:
       ibogaine is only weakly psychedelic in the classical sense
     → The visions are more "documentary" than "kaleidoscopic"
     → You don't see sacred geometry — you see your childhood

  5. SIGMA-2 RECEPTOR — Agonist
     Ki ~0.09 uM (significant affinity)
     → Sigma-2 modulates calcium signaling, apoptosis, motor function
     → Implicated in "dreamy" dissociative states
     → May contribute to the "watching a movie" quality of the
       life review — the characteristic ibogaine detachment
     → Sigma-2 ligands under investigation for neurodegenerative
       diseases (cognitive enhancement, neuroprotection)

  6. SEROTONIN TRANSPORTER (SERT) / DOPAMINE TRANSPORTER (DAT)
     → Ibogaine inhibits both transporters (reuptake inhibition)
     → Noribogaine has stronger SERT affinity (~0.04 uM)
     → Combined SERT/DAT modulation = mood stabilization during
       the integration period
     → This is why people report "emotional equanimity" for weeks
       after ibogaine — noribogaine is still modulating monoamines

THE POLY-PHARMACOLOGY PRINCIPLE:
  Ibogaine's therapeutic power comes from engaging ALL these systems
  simultaneously. No single receptor explains the effect. The ordeal
  emerges from the CONCERT of:
    - KOR dysphoria (you can't escape into pleasure)
    - NMDA reset (your conditioned responses dissolve)
    - nAChR blockade (the reward switch turns off)
    - 5-HT2A activation (mild visionary access)
    - Sigma-2 (detached witnessing perspective)
    - SERT/DAT modulation (emotional stabilization)

  This is pharmacological initiation: the molecule sets up conditions
  where healing MUST occur because every escape route is blocked.
```

### Cardiac Risk — The Price of the Ordeal

```
QT PROLONGATION — THE LETHAL SHADOW:
  Ibogaine blocks hERG (human ether-a-go-go-related gene) potassium
  channels in cardiac myocytes.

  Mechanism:
  → hERG channels mediate rapid delayed rectifier current (IKr)
  → IKr is essential for cardiac repolarization
  → Block IKr → prolonged QT interval on ECG
  → Prolonged QT → risk of Torsades de Pointes (polymorphic
    ventricular tachycardia) → sudden cardiac death

  Risk factors:
  → Pre-existing QT prolongation
  → Electrolyte imbalances (hypokalemia, hypomagnesemia)
  → Concurrent medications that prolong QT
  → Structural heart disease
  → CYP2D6 poor metabolizer status (higher ibogaine exposure)

  Reported fatalities:
  → ~30 deaths associated with ibogaine (1990-2020)
  → Most in unsupervised settings without cardiac monitoring
  → Many had pre-existing cardiac conditions or concurrent drugs
  → In medically supervised settings with screening: risk is LOW
    but NOT zero (Noller et al., 2018: 0 deaths in 51 observed cases
    in New Zealand with medical supervision)

  MANDATORY PROTOCOL:
  → 12-lead ECG pre-screening (QTc must be < 450ms for males,
    < 470ms for females)
  → Continuous cardiac telemetry during entire experience
  → IV access maintained
  → Electrolyte panel (K+, Mg2+, Ca2+)
  → Full medication reconciliation (no QT-prolonging drugs)
  → Medical team on standby for minimum 48 hours

  THE METAPHORICAL TRUTH:
    The cardiac risk IS the teaching. Initiation carries real danger.
    The Bwiti know this. The nganga knows this. Howard Lotsof knew
    this. Genuine transformation is not safe. It must be approached
    with absolute seriousness, proper preparation, and expert guidance.
    Anyone who tells you transformation is comfortable and risk-free
    is selling you something — and it isn't transformation.
```
