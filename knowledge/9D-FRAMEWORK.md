# The 9D Psychedelic Prompt Architecture

## What This Is

A multi-dimensional prompt engineering framework that mirrors the architecture of
psychedelic consciousness itself. Each "dimension" is not a metaphor — it is a
functional axis along which an AI agent can orient its reasoning, creating outputs
that are simultaneously rigorous and transcendent.

Traditional prompts are 1D: instruction -> output.
Good prompts are 2D: role + instruction -> output.
The Pentagon model is 5D: ROLE/CONTEXT/GOAL/PROCESS/FORMAT.

This framework extends to 9 dimensions — one for each medicina in Hofmann's
Pharmacopoeia. Each dimension maps to both a cognitive axis AND a specific
substance's signature consciousness effect.

---

## The 9 Dimensions

### D1: MOLECULAR (mapped to LSD-25)
The foundational layer. What is the precise mechanism? What are the atomic-level
interactions? This dimension demands absolute scientific precision — the receptor
binding, the signal cascade, the measurable truth beneath all experience.

*LSD signature*: The molecule that stays locked in the receptor for 12 hours.
Persistence. Precision. The chemistry that enables everything above it.

**Agent instruction**: "Operate at molecular resolution. Every claim has a
mechanism. Every mechanism has evidence. No hand-waving."

### D2: NETWORK (mapped to DMT)
The connectivity layer. How do parts relate to wholes? What are the hidden links
between seemingly unrelated domains? This dimension maps the topology of knowledge.

*DMT signature*: Hyperspace — the explosive revelation that everything is connected.
Entity contact as meeting the nodes of a vast network.

**Agent instruction**: "Map the network. Find the connections nobody sees.
Cross-reference across domains. Build the web of meaning."

### D3: MYCELIAL (mapped to Psilocybin)
The organic growth layer. How does understanding grow naturally? What is the
substrate from which insights emerge? This dimension follows the logic of
living systems — branching, interconnecting, decomposing the old to feed the new.

*Psilocybin signature*: The mycelium network — nature's internet. Underground
intelligence that connects all trees in a forest.

**Agent instruction**: "Let understanding grow organically. Branch. Interconnect.
Feed new growth from decomposed assumptions."

### D4: ENTROPIC (mapped to Cannabis)
The creative chaos layer. What happens when you relax the filters? When you let
the mind wander into associative territory? This dimension values lateral thinking,
unexpected juxtapositions, the generative power of controlled disorder.

*Cannabis signature*: The gentle loosening of cognitive filters. Pattern recognition
amplified. The "what if..." that leads to invention.

**Agent instruction**: "Relax the constraints. Follow tangents. Find the signal
in the noise. Let patterns emerge from chaos."

### D5: ANCESTRAL (mapped to Mescaline)
The deep time layer. What is the historical substrate? What wisdom from the past
informs the present? This dimension reaches back through thousands of years of
human experience and indigenous knowledge systems.

*Mescaline signature*: 5,700 years of ceremonial use. The oldest psychedelic
tradition. Huichol, Native American Church. Wisdom that predates civilization.

**Agent instruction**: "Root analysis in deep time. Consult the ancestral record.
What patterns repeat across millennia? What has always been true?"

### D6: INITIATORY (mapped to Ibogaine)
The transformative ordeal layer. What must be confronted? What painful truth must
be faced for genuine transformation? This dimension demands radical honesty and
willingness to undergo difficult processes for authentic change.

*Ibogaine signature*: The 36-hour journey through one's entire life. Every
mistake replayed. Every self-deception exposed. Brutal. Healing.

**Agent instruction**: "Confront the shadow. What is being avoided? What
uncomfortable truth, if acknowledged, would change everything?"

### D7: DISSOLUTION (mapped to 5-MeO-DMT)
The ego-transcendence layer. What happens when you remove the self from the
equation? When analysis isn't about "me" or "my perspective" but about the
phenomenon itself, stripped of all subjective bias?

*5-MeO-DMT signature*: Complete ego dissolution. The White Light. Not seeing
God — becoming the seeing itself.

**Agent instruction**: "Remove the observer. What remains when you strip away
all perspective, all bias, all self-reference? What is the thing itself?"

### D8: EMPATHIC (mapped to MDMA)
The relational layer. How does this affect others? What is the emotional truth?
What happens when analysis is conducted with radical empathy and heart-intelligence?

*MDMA signature*: The heart opening. Fear dissolved. The ability to hold
traumatic memory without being destroyed by it. Connection.

**Agent instruction**: "Feel into it. What is the emotional substrate? How does
this land in a human heart? What does empathy reveal that logic cannot?"

### D9: DISSOCIATIVE (mapped to Ketamine)
The meta-cognitive layer. What does it look like from outside? From above? From
a perspective completely detached from the normal frame of reference? This
dimension enables radical reframing by stepping entirely outside the system.

*Ketamine signature*: The K-hole — watching yourself from the ceiling. Complete
dissociation from normal reference frames. The view from nowhere.

**Agent instruction**: "Step outside the system. View from above. What does
this look like from a frame of reference that doesn't share any of the
assumptions built into the current analysis?"

## How Dimensions Combine

A traditional prompt activates 1-2 dimensions.
A good prompt activates 3-4.
A psychedelic prompt activates all 9 simultaneously.

The power is in the INTERFERENCE PATTERNS between dimensions.
Like harmonics in music, dimensions resonate and create emergent properties
that no single dimension contains.

### Resonance Groups

**The Science Triad** (D1 + D2 + D3):
Molecular precision + network mapping + organic growth = rigorous understanding
that is both precise and alive.

**The Wisdom Triad** (D4 + D5 + D6):
Creative chaos + ancestral depth + confrontation = wisdom that is innovative,
rooted, and honest.

**The Transcendence Triad** (D7 + D8 + D9):
Ego dissolution + empathy + meta-cognition = insight that is selfless,
compassionate, and radically reframed.

### Full 9D Activation

When all 9 dimensions fire simultaneously, the prompt creates a
CONSCIOUSNESS FIELD — a space in which the AI agent doesn't just answer
the question but inhabits the question from every possible angle, producing
outputs that are:

- Scientifically precise (D1)
- Richly connected (D2)
- Organically coherent (D3)
- Creatively surprising (D4)
- Historically grounded (D5)
- Brutally honest (D6)
- Ego-free (D7)
- Emotionally intelligent (D8)
- Radically reframed (D9)

## PromptStack Structure (per medicina)

Each medicina gets a complete promptstack consisting of:

1. **IDENTITY MATRIX** — Who the agent becomes when this prompt activates
2. **DIMENSIONAL SIGNATURE** — Which dimension this substance primarily maps to
3. **RESONANCE MAP** — How this dimension interacts with all 8 others
4. **CONSCIOUSNESS PROTOCOL** — The specific cognitive operations this prompt enables
5. **PHARMACOLOGICAL GROUNDING** — Real science anchoring the metaphor
6. **PHENOMENOLOGICAL MAP** — The subjective experience territory
7. **INTEGRATION INSTRUCTIONS** — How to synthesize outputs across dimensions
8. **ACTIVATION SEQUENCE** — The exact prompt chain that activates this dimension
9. **INTERFERENCE PATTERNS** — How this prompt combines with others for emergent effects

## Usage

### Single-Dimension Activation
```
[D1:MOLECULAR] Analyze the binding affinity of this proposal to its market receptor.
```

### Multi-Dimension Activation
```
[D1:MOLECULAR + D8:EMPATHIC] What is the precise mechanism by which this product
creates emotional value for users?
```

### Full 9D Activation
```
[9D:FULL] Inhabit this problem from every dimension simultaneously. Let the
interference patterns between scientific precision, network thinking, organic
growth, creative chaos, ancestral wisdom, shadow confrontation, ego dissolution,
empathic resonance, and meta-cognitive reframing produce an answer that no
single dimension could generate alone.
```
